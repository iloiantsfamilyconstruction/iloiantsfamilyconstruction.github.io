FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Here are some of the most common things our customers ask us.

What types of stone do you work with?

We work with granite, marble, quartz, travertine, limestone, slate, quartzite, onyx, and many other natural and engineered stone varieties. We'll help you find the perfect stone for your project's aesthetic and functional needs.

Do you offer in-store and in-home consultations?

Yes! We offer both in-store visits at our showroom and free in-home consultations. We'll assess your space, present material samples, and provide a detailed custom quote at no charge.

How long does a typical countertop installation take?

Most countertop projects are completed within 15-25 days from template to installation, depending on the scope. We'll provide a specific timeline during your consultation based on your project's complexity.

Can you service and repair existing stone features?

Absolutely. We offer full service, restoration, sealing, and repair for stone fireplaces, countertops, vanities, and other stone features — even if we didn't originally install them.

Do you handle full kitchen or bathroom remodels?

Yes! ILOIANTS handles complete kitchen and bathroom remodels including cabinets, countertops, flooring, fixtures, and all stonework — a true one-stop shop for your renovation needs.

Are you licensed and insured in California?

Yes, ILOIANTS Family Construction is fully licensed, bonded, and insured in the state of California. We comply with all local building codes and regulations to protect you and your home.

Do you provide warranties on your work?

Yes. All our installations come with a craftsmanship warranty. We stand behind the quality of our work and will address any issues that arise from our installation at no additional cost.

How do I care for and maintain natural stone?

We provide care instructions with every installation. Generally, natural stone should be sealed periodically and cleaned with pH-neutral cleaners. We also offer professional sealing and maintenance services to keep your stone looking its best.
